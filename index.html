Beat The Myth

Donating blood is safe and simple. It takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete the blood donation process.
You walk into a reputed and safe blood donation centre.
Then a quick physical check will be done to check temperature,BP etc to ensure you are a healthy donor.
If found fit to donate.Approximately 350 ml of blood will be collected in one donation.
The blood is now ready to be taken to the hospital,to save lives.

DO donate blood, only if you satisfy all of the following conditions

You are between age group of 18-60 years. Your weight is 45 kgs or more.
Your hemoglobin is 12.5 gm% minimum.
Your last blood donation was 3 or more months earlier.
